# 📨 Soft Launch — Inner Circle Outreach Templates

> **Goal:** Get 50 devs to star the repo, try the product, and give honest feedback before public launch.  
> **Timing:** Send 1-2 weeks before public launch day.  
> **Method:** DM on X/Twitter, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or email — whatever is most personal.

---

## Template 1: Close Developer Friends (Personal DM)

```
Hey [Name]!

I've been heads-down building something for the past months and I'm about to launch it publicly. Before I do, I'd love your honest take.

It's called Stoic AgentOS — basically a command center for AI agents. If you're running agents in production (or plan to), it gives you real-time monitoring, persistent memory, and audit logs. 3-line SDK integration.

Would mean the world if you could:
1. ⭐ Star the repo: https://github.com/benjaminkernbaum-ux/stoic-agentos
2. Try the free tier: https://stoicagentos.com/signup
3. Tell me honestly what sucks / what's confusing / what's missing

No pressure to be nice — I need the real feedback before public launch.

Thanks! 🙏
```

## Targeting Strategy

### Who to reach out to (priority order):
1. **Developers you personally know** who use AI agents (highest conversion)
2. **X/Twitter mutuals** who post about AI/agents/LLMs
3. **LinkedIn 1st connections** in engineering/AI roles
4. **Open-source maintainers** of related projects (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen contributors)
5. **Dev community members** from Hacker News, Indie Hackers, etc.

### When to send:
- **Ideal:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM their local time
- **Batch 1 (25 people):** Send 7-10 days before launch
- **Batch 2 (25 people):** Send 3-5 days before launch
- **Follow-up:** If no response in 3 days, send a casual nudge

### What to ask for:
- ⭐ **Star the repo** (lowest friction, highest impact)
- 🧪 **Try the signup** (medium friction, catches UX bugs)
- 💬 **DM feedback** (highest value, lowest volume)
- 🔄 **RT/share on launch day** (ask this closer to launch)
